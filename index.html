Linus Sommer

About

I am a mathematics master's student at Leipzig University, with a keen interest in computational methods. Currently, writing my master's thesis in the MATHEXP group at INRIA Saclay, focusing on computer algebra. Prior to this, I collaborated with Paul Breiding at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (MPI MiS) on computational algebraic geometry.

Previously, I have also worked at the Max Planck Institute for Human Cognitive and Brain Sciences (MPI CBS), the Max Planck Institute for Dynamics of Complex Technical Systems (MPI Magdeburg) and the Fraunhofer Center for Silicon Photovoltaics (Fraunhofer CSP), and very briefly in consulting (KPMG AG).
